🥐 Resource Variability

Butter Bread Production - Quality Management Task 3

Explore

Raw Materials

🦠 Yeast

Type of Variability

  • Concentration (2–3%)
  • Temperature (30–35°C)
  • Proofing Time (60 min)

Impact on Process Consistency

  • Under fermentation
  • Over fermentation
  • Irregular gas production

Impact on Product Quality

  • Poor loaf volume
  • Dense crumb
  • Reduced aroma

Other Impacts

  • Material loss
  • Higher production cost

🌾 Flour

Type of Variability

  • Water-to-flour ratio
  • Protein concentration

Impact on Process Consistency

  • Weak dough structure
  • Sticky dough

Impact on Product Quality

  • Poor loaf shape
  • Reduced shelf life

Other Impacts

  • Machinery sticking
  • Dough tearing

🧈 Butter

Type of Variability

  • SMP 32–35°C
  • FFA spikes
  • Fat ratio fluctuation

Impact on Process Consistency

  • Difficult moulding
  • Conveyor tearing

Impact on Product Quality

  • Dry bread
  • Loss of softness
  • Poor aroma

Other Impacts

  • Waste generation
  • Microbial growth risk

Utilities

⚡ Electricity

Variability

  • Voltage fluctuation
  • Power interruption

Process Impact

  • Unstable mixers
  • Unstable proofers
  • Unstable ovens

Product Quality Impact

  • Uneven loaf volume
  • Inconsistent crust colour
  • Underbaked bread

Other Impacts

  • Downtime
  • Maintenance costs

❄ Refrigeration

Variability

  • Temperature fluctuation
  • Uneven cooling rate

Process Impact

  • Unstable storage
  • Variable cooling

Product Quality Impact

  • Reduced freshness
  • Soggy texture
  • Condensation

Other Impacts

  • Spoilage risk
  • Higher energy usage

🌬 HVAC

Variability

  • Temperature fluctuation
  • Humidity variation
  • Poor circulation

Process Impact

  • Inconsistent fermentation
  • Variable proofing time

Product Quality Impact

  • Poor loaf volume
  • Irregular texture

Other Impacts

  • Mold growth
  • Hygiene issues

Equipment

⚙ Mixers & Kneaders

Variability

  • Mixing below 8 min
  • Mixing above 15 min

Process Impact

  • Weak gluten development
  • Dough overheating

Product Quality Impact

  • Dense crumb
  • Poor texture

Other Impacts

  • Batch rejection
  • Energy wastage

🧪 Fermentation Tanks

Variability

  • 30–35°C temperature
  • 75–85% RH humidity

Process Impact

  • Slow fermentation
  • Over fermentation

Product Quality Impact

  • Reduced loaf volume
  • Poor loaf shape

Other Impacts

  • Food waste
  • Reduced consistency

🔥 Industrial Ovens

Variability

  • 180–200°C temperature
  • 25–35 min baking time

Process Impact

  • Incomplete baking
  • Excessive drying

Product Quality Impact

  • Pale crust
  • Dark crust
  • Dry texture

Other Impacts

  • Energy consumption
  • Product rejection

Skilled Personnel

👨‍🔬 Food Technologist

Type of Variability

  • Insufficient bakery process knowledge
  • Incorrect dough temperature control (26–28°C)
  • Incorrect proofing temperature setting (35–40°C)

Impact on Process Consistency

  • Inconsistent yeast activity
  • Uneven dough expansion
  • Variable fermentation performance

Impact on Product Quality

  • Low loaf volume
  • Dense crumb structure
  • Uneven crust colour
  • Reduced softness

Other Impacts

  • Batch-to-batch variation
  • Reduced production consistency

👨‍💼 Production Supervisor

Type of Variability

  • Failure to monitor proofing temperature (35–40°C)
  • Poor humidity control (75–85% RH)
  • Irregular workflow supervision

Impact on Process Consistency

  • Uneven proofing
  • Inconsistent baking performance
  • Production flow disruptions

Impact on Product Quality

  • Under-proofed or over-proofed bread
  • Inconsistent texture
  • Non-uniform loaf quality

Other Impacts

  • Reduced productivity
  • Customer dissatisfaction

✅ Quality Control Operator

Type of Variability

  • Insufficient GMP & HACCP competency
  • Moisture content deviation
  • Texture testing inconsistency

Impact on Process Consistency

  • Defective batches not detected
  • Inconsistent quality verification
  • Unreliable inspection results

Impact on Product Quality

  • Reduced shelf life
  • Inconsistent texture and freshness
  • Variable product quality

Other Impacts

  • Product recall risk
  • Brand reputation damage

Conclusion

Resource variability directly affects process consistency, product quality, production efficiency, and operating costs. Effective monitoring is essential for consistent butter bread production.
